San Diego Oven & Stove Repair

When your oven or stove stops working, cooking becomes impossible. A-Tech Repair provides expert oven and range repair with proven skills for long-lasting solutions.

We repair both gas and electric ovens, stoves, ranges, and cooktops. From standard ranges to high-end Wolf and Viking appliances, our technicians have the experience to fix it right.

Common Oven Problems We Fix

  • Oven not heating
  • Uneven cooking
  • Temperature inaccuracy
  • Self-cleaning function not working
  • Oven door won't close
  • Control panel issues
  • Igniter problems (gas)
  • Element failures (electric)

Common Stove/Cooktop Problems

  • Burners not lighting
  • Weak flame
  • Electric burner not heating
  • Cracked cooktop glass
  • Knob malfunctions

Brands We Service

Wolf, Viking, Thermador, GE, Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, KitchenAid, Bosch, Frigidaire, and all other major brands.
