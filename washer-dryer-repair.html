San Diego Washer & Dryer Repair

A broken washer or dryer disrupts your entire household. A-Tech Repair provides quick, professional laundry appliance repair to get you back to your routine fast.

Our technicians are trained on all washer and dryer types, from standard top-loaders to high-efficiency front-load models. We service both gas and electric dryers.

  • Not draining properly
  • Leaking water
  • Excessive vibration
  • Not filling with water

  • Error codes

Common Dryer Problems We Fix

  • Taking too long to dry
  • Making unusual noises
  • Not tumbling
  • Shutting off mid-cycle
  • Gas dryer won't ignite
